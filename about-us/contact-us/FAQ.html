Frequently Asked Questions:

  • In how many cities is mycart.pk operational?

    • Cash&Carry is available all over Pakistan. We provide free delivery in Karachi, Lahore and Islamabad. However, we do charge a very nominal logistic fee if a shipping address is beyond the delivery area of Karachi, Lahore and Islamabad.

  • Are there any Delivery Charges?

    • Mycart.pk ensures on-time delivery despite of conditional hazards and hurdles. For this exclusive service we charge a very nominal fee with respect to order amount and weight. Below are the charges details:

    Shipping Charges Weight
    Flat Rs. 200/- Under 20kg
    *Rs. 50/- will be charged additionally for every 5 kg weight exceeding 20 KG E.g. Order weight is 30kg, shipping charges of Rs. 300/- will be charged ***Courier charges will be charged for deliveries other than Karachi, Lahore & Islamabad.

  • What is Cash&Carry Account?

    • Cash&Carry Account is created at the time of sign-up. With creating your personal Cash&Carry Account, you can keep a track of your orders, Reward Points, Store Credit.

  • How can I create my Account on Cash&Carry.pk?

    • Account creation on Cash&Carry.pk is hassle free procedure. Just fill in the basic details which are marked mandatory under the asterisk sign (*) and the website will send you an Activation Code on your mobile number and once verified, your account is created with us.
