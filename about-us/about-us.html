About Us

Cash&Carry.pk provides a one-stop, enhanced shopping experience to consumers online, for their daily grocery needs and planned purchases of housewares, home textiles, toys, gifts, etc. Currently it operates in Karachi offering 24/7 deliveries and customer support with short-term plans to expand its operations nationwide.


Cash&Carry.pk operates it’s own dedicated and stocked fulfilment centre along with a fleet of vehicles to provide express deliveries, daily time slotted deliveries and overnight order fulfilment.


Cash&Carry.pk has already forged valuable business relationships with majority of brands in CPG category and is moving rapidly to expanding the number of SKU’s available for its customers
